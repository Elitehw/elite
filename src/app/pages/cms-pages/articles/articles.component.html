SUBTITLE I A

Alaska's extreme climate and expansive landscape are thrilling but also present real challenges to homeowners. Homes are impacted by seasons of wind, deep freeze and energy access issues. This takes its toll, shortening the lifespan of common home systems and appliances.

Alaskan homes are built with internal mechanisms to insulate and protect residents from the extreme weather. Arctic issues require that electrical and mechanical devices in the typical Alaskan home meet certain specifications for function and utility. In other words, if you live in Alaska, you need the highest quality heating system, water heaters and plumbing systems to protect you from the elements.

Even strong, well-built Alaskan homes are susceptible. It's natural that structural issues, moisture, plumbing and strain on heating systems is an expected part of owning a home in Alaska. For these and other issues, an Elite Home warranty agreement can help.

For homeowners in Alaska, Elite Home Warranty offers plans that cover essential home systems and appliances, including those especially crucial to life in this state. We cover HVAC, heating and cooling systems, appliances such as a water heater and washer/dryer, plumbing and much more. When you get an Elite Home Warranty plan, you get absolute assurance that you will be covered if one of these costly items breaks down. With environmental conditions that necessitate the uninterrupted operation of your plumbing or heating, we have you covered.
