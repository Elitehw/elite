Home Warranty Coverage

Home warranties are not the same as....

Home warranties are not the same as homeowners insurance. Insurance protects you from natural disasters, fires, floods and similar catastrophes. The more likely catastrophe is that an appliance or system in your home will break down. Home warranties aren’t required but they are considered essential by many homeowners
