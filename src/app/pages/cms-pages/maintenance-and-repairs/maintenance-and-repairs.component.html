Home Maintenance

Even with a great warranty plan to fall back on, homeowners should maintain appliances and systems in the home. Regular, appropriate maintenance will naturally keep everything running better. This diminishes the need to file claims at all.
Because home warranties often cover homes of any age, a lack of maintenance is a huge issue. During a claim filing process, a home warranty company may request maintenance records. Even if they initially covered an item, a clear lack of recommended maintenance could nullify that coverage
